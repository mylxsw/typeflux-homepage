After months of development and internal testing, we're excited to share Typeflux with you. It's a voice-input tool built for macOS users — born from a personal need, designed for everyone. Press the Fn key, speak naturally, and let us handle the rest.

## What's in this release

- **One-click voice input**: Hold the Fn key to start speaking, release to stop. No need to switch input methods, no buttons to click — it works in any text field, anywhere
- **Local speech recognition**: Supports on-device models. Fast, accurate, and works without an internet connection. Your voice data never leaves your Mac
- **Voice Agent**: Press Fn + Space and talk to AI by voice. Ask questions, rewrite text, translate content, or handle more complex tasks through natural conversation
- **Personas for every scene**: Built-in presets for work, study, and social scenarios. You can also create your own persona to make speech recognition fit your exact context
- **Privacy by design**: Open-source code, local processing, zero data collection. Your voice belongs to you and no one else

## A few things to know

This is a v0.1.0 release, which means it's still in early testing. Features are evolving quickly, and you might run into the occasional rough edge. If you spot a bug or have an idea, we'd love to hear from you via GitHub Issues.

macOS only for now. We'll keep improving based on your feedback.
